About Us

Carshare Incorporation aims to provide carpooling facilities throughout Kerala. Currently operating in the city of Kochi, our primary aim to reduce the pollutin and the traffic nuisance caused due to the ever increasing population of cars in the city. We aim to make Kochi much more cleaner and greener. We encourage all Kochites to play their part in the reduction of pollution and congestion in our city.
